Requirements Checklist

Ensure you have everything ready before registering.

  • Active Student ID (KTM)

    Must be currently enrolled in an Indonesian university.

  • Team Registration

    Teams can consist of 1 to 3 members from the same university.

  • Registration Fee Payment

    Upload valid payment proof during the registration process.

    Signed document stating the codebase is your team's original work.
