Help Center

How can we help?

Registration
Can I register individually without a team?

Yes! You can register individually or form a team of up to 3 members. Individual participants will be judged on the exact same criteria as teams.

Requirements
Do I need to be a Computer Science student?

No, NovaMind is open to all active university students in Indonesia regardless of their major, as long as they can develop web applications.

Payment
What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bank transfers (BCA, Mandiri, BNI, BRI) and popular e-wallets like GoPay, OVO, and Dana. Please upload the receipt during registration.

Submission
Where do we host our live website?

You can use any free hosting provider like Vercel, Netlify, GitHub Pages, or a custom VPS. Ensure the link is accessible without a password.

Requirements
Can we use CSS frameworks like Tailwind?

For the Dynamic and Framework categories, yes. However, for the Static Website category, you are expected to write raw CSS to demonstrate foundational skills.

Registration
What happens if we miss Batch 1?

You can still register in Batch 2. The main difference is the registration fee, which may be slightly higher in Batch 2.
